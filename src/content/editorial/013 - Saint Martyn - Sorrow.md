The first thing that caught my attention was the drum machines, they sound deeply unsettling, almost like a military march. Dark synths and quiet, almost whispered vocals create an even more tense atmosphere. At one point, the intensity builds to the breaking point, erupting into a scream and a guitar breakdown—as if anxiety is transforming into aggression. Written in response to the exclusion of trans voices from the debate of the "For Women Scotland" case, the track is imbued with anger — sharp and focused.
