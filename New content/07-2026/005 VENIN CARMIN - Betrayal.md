 VENIN CARMIN blends post-punk, coldwave, and dark pop into a sharp, high-energy track driven by punchy rhythms and commanding female vocals.

Betrayal turns the sting of broken trust into something cold, confident, and irresistibly catchy, balancing danceable momentum with emotional tension. 
 
Rather than dwelling on heartbreak, the song confronts it head-on with striking clarity and attitude.
