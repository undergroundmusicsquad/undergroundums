I particularly enjoyed the reversed vocal effects and layered backing vocals, which to my ear evoke classic dream pop, especially artists like Cocteau Twins and Julee Cruise. The guitar solos, shimmering backgrounds, and organic sound of the violins gradually bring in psychedelic elements. And just when it seems like the track is about to settle into a particular mood, the rhythm shifts again and making me wonder what will come next.
