At first, the combination of the lyrics and the guitars' anxious mood seemed mismatched to me, but the line “grilling my grief in the beef” triggered a complete shift in context that made me want to listen to the song again; I got the impression it was about suppressing emotions and scorning displays of muscular bravado. The minor-key guitars create repetitive patterns — each pattern followed by a change in rhythm — while backing vocals emphasize specific words, creating a sense of pressure.
